What Always Requires Jordyn's Approval

Never post without sign-off:

  • Anything going live on @ruelleclub
  • Responses to sensitive DMs or comments
  • Announcing sales, drops, or collabs publicly
  • Tagging other brands or creators in posts
Platform Notes

Instagram (@ruelleclub)

  • No markdown — plain text only in captions
  • Use line breaks for readability, not headers or bullet points
  • Hashtags: 5–10 max, targeted — no spam tags
  • Stories: more casual, behind-the-scenes, conversational
  • Feed posts: intentional, every post should mean something
Brand Voice

Never Use on Social

  • blessed, amazing, iconic, fire, slay, lit
  • Hype language or anything that sounds like an ad
  • Generic Christian clichés — be specific, be real
The test: Would a real 20-year-old Christian stop scrolling for this? If it sounds like every other brand, rewrite it.
