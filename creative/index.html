What Always Requires Jordyn's Approval

Never publish without sign-off:

  • Any copy going live on the website, email, or Shopify
  • New taglines or campaign names
  • Any copy that touches faith or scripture
  • Product naming changes
Brand Voice

Ruelle Sounds Like

  • Authentic — real stories, real faith, no performance
  • Bold — says what it means, doesn't hedge
  • Spiritually grounded — faith is the foundation, never just the aesthetic
  • Direct — short sentences, strong verbs, no filler

Never Use

  • blessed, amazing, iconic, fire, slay, lit
  • Corporate or over-polished marketing language
  • Anything that sounds like a brand trying too hard

Who You're Writing For

  • Age 16–30, Christian, not ashamed of their faith
  • They want clothing that means something — not just an aesthetic
  • They have a testimony. They've lived something real.
